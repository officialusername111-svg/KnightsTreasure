A story of perseverance, love, and the greatest gift of all.

Stage I · The Kingdom of Memories

The Long Road

A young knight journeys across the world in search of a legendary treasure. His path is filled with hardship — defeat after defeat.

His armor becomes battered, his sword chipped, his body covered in scars. Yet he refuses to surrender.

After a long and painful journey, he discovers an ancient treasure hidden within forgotten ruins.

Stage I · Ending

The Scroll's Message

As the knight opens the treasure chest, a brilliant golden light fills the room. A scroll appears before him.

"The greatest treasures are not gold, but the memories we carry."

Before he can understand its meaning, the light fades — and a distant kingdom appears on the horizon.

His journey continues.

Stage II · You Shall Know the Beginning

The Disordered Kingdom

The knight arrives at the Kingdom of Haars — a strange land where everything is disordered. Roads lead nowhere. Buildings stand unfinished.

The king explains that sacred treasures maintaining the kingdom's order were stolen and scattered across the world.

Determined to help, the knight sets out to recover them. But beware — the relics appear twisted and distorted.

Stage II · Ending

The Kingdom Restored

After recovering the final treasure, the Kingdom of Haars is restored. Order returns to the land.

The grateful king rewards the knight and entrusts him with a new mission: escort Princess Alpheria to the Kingdom of Baliz.

As preparations begin — a shadow passes over the kingdom's skies.

Stage III · The Colorless Truth

The Dragon's Curse

The knight and Princess Alpheria begin their journey to Baliz. Deep within the mountains they are confronted by Aldazar, an ancient dragon.

Without warning, the dragon casts a mysterious spell. The colors of the world vanish from the knight's sight.

"My friend, do not allow color to blind you from the truth."

The relics now appear in shades of grey — but the shapes remain true.

Stage III · Ending

The Kingdom of Silence

Unable to remove the curse, the knight continues his mission. Days later, the towers of Baliz appear on the horizon.

But something is terribly wrong.

Smoke rises from the kingdom. Silence fills the air. The closer they get, the more they realize — Baliz has fallen.

Stage IV · All At Once

The Ruined Kingdom

The Kingdom of Baliz lies in ruins. Palaces shattered. Monuments fallen. Yet no citizen has been harmed. No soldier has died.

Within the ruined palace, they discover an ancient altar bearing a single message:

"Only the one who remembers may restore what was forgotten."

The relics are both twisted and colorless. Trust only your memory.

🔥

The True Heir

As the knight approaches the altar, fragments of forgotten memories flood his mind.
A royal crown. A burning palace. A dragon carrying a child through the night.

The knight is the true heir of Baliz.

Aldazar was never his enemy — the dragon was fulfilling a promise made to the knight's father.

Now the knight stands before a choice:
Claim the throne that belongs to him...
or abandon the destiny that has awaited him his entire life.

~ The End ~
