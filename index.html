A story of perseverance, love, and the greatest gift of all.

Chapter I

The Long Road

The young knight had traveled for what felt like a lifetime. His armor was cracked. His sword was chipped. Countless scars covered his body.

Many nights he lay wounded beneath the cold sky, wondering if he had the strength to continue. Fear walked beside him. Doubt whispered in his ear. Failure followed his every step.

Yet still, he moved forward.

Chapter II

The Dark Cave

One day, exhausted beyond measure, he arrived before a dark cave hidden deep within the mountains.

The knight gazed into its endless darkness.

"Perhaps this is where my journey ends."

With nothing left to lose, he entered. The cave was silent. Cold. Ancient.

As he ventured deeper, he felt a strange force guiding him — something unseen calling his name, gently pushing him forward.

Chapter III

The Magnificent Chest

At last, he reached the center of the cave. His legs gave way. His body was broken. His spirit nearly shattered.

As he prepared to surrender, a faint golden light appeared before him. There, resting upon a stone pedestal, stood a magnificent treasure chest.

Gold shimmered across its surface. Precious jewels sparkled like stars. Gathering the last of his strength, the knight approached — his trembling hands reached for the lid.

Slowly... Carefully... He opened it.

Chapter IV

The Message Within

A brilliant light burst from the chest, filling the entire cave. From within the light, a scroll slowly rose into the air. The knight carefully unrolled the ancient parchment — and words began to appear...

💛

Hello, Bi —

Happy Birthday. Enjoy your day, subong. You are the most beautiful blessing that has ever found its way to me.

Ikaw ang pinakamaganda nga regalo nga nag-abot sa akon. Wala ko sang gina-expect nga makilala ko ikaw, pero subong nga natabuan ta, indi ko na mapangita pa ang iban. Ikaw na ang akon.

You are the one who supports me, always — in every shadow and in every light. You are the treasure I found in the moments I didn't even know I deserved someone like you.

Permi ka naga-support sa akon, Bi. Sa tanan nga oras — bisan pag maguol ako, bisan pag nagalaglag ang tanan sa palibot ko — naga-remain ka. Daw suga ka sa kalipunggan. Daw tubig ka sa mainit nga adlaw. Indi ko mahunahuna kung ano ang akon nga buhay kung wala ka.

✦

Salamat gid, Bi. I love you so much.

I am so proud of you — of everything you do, everything you are, everything you are still becoming. You deserve every good thing that arrives at your door, and I believe with all my heart that more of it is on its way.

Proud gid ko sa imo, Bi. Sa mga gina-himu mo, sa mga gina-achieve mo, sa tawo nga nagapadayon ka nga mangin. Kabalo ko nga lisud ang dalan — pero permi mo ginapakita nga kaya mo. Kag kaya ko man, tungod sa imo.

Stand tall, always. And if anyone tries to dim your light — remember who you are. Fight for it. Dapat mag-palag ka sa mga kalaban mo, Bi. Indi ka magpabully. Indi ka magpasaylo sa mga tawo nga wala nagpalangga sa imo pareho sang gina-palangga ko sa imo.

✦

I'm sorry I could not give you something grand. No gift wrapped in ribbon, no treasure made of gold. But I made you this — with all the effort and love I carry. It is humble. But it is honest.

Sorry gid, Bi. Wala ko sang regalo nga mahimu ko ihatag sa imo subong. Nagasakit man akon dughan nga wala ako sing mahatag sa imo sa imo espesyal nga adlaw. Pero kabalo ka nga kung mayroon lang ako sang paagi, buhaton ko tanan para sa imo. Amu ini ang akon nahimu — ginbutang ko diri ang tanan nga ginabatyag ko para sa imo.

✦

Thank you for everything. For the care, the laughter, the late-night talks, the way you push me forward when I forget how to move. Too many things to name — but every single one lives in my heart.

Salamat sa care mo, sa mga bonding ta, sa mga usapan ta sa gab-i nga wala na sang oras. Salamat sa mga pagkakataon nga gintudluan mo ako nga magpareho sa imo kabuotan. Salamat kay permi kang nag-abot para sa akon — bisan pag indi ko deserving.

✦

Damo pa ang indi ko masiling, Bi. Kulang ang mga pulong para ihulagway ang tanan nga ginabatyag ko para sa imo. Pero kabalo ka — nahibaloan mo na yan. Nabatyagan mo na yan. Kag amu na siguro ang pinakamatahum nga parte sini tanan.

I am so blessed that you are my partner. Ang kalipay ko gid nga ikaw ang akon.

Grow and continue your journey,
my Precious Treasure.
Padayon lang, Bi. Permi ko ikaw palangga-on.

— With love, always 🔥

✦ My Treasure ✦

Chapter V

The True Treasure

The light that burst from the chest did not fade. It grew — spilling out of the cave, over the mountains, across the frozen kingdom.

The knight stood. For the first time in years, the weight on his shoulders was gone.

He had faced every darkness. He had fallen and risen. He had walked through the coldest nights alone — and found, at the end of all of it, that the greatest treasure was never gold.

"It was the love that waited for him at the end of the road."

And so the knight, battered and scarred, finally smiled.
